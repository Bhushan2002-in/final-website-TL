Why in person and not online?

There is a difference between efficient and effective. Yes, online Zoom meetings/lessons are highly efficient, but they are nowhere near as effective as an in-person event. I want to give you REAL value and make sure we get the most from our time together, so we’re choosing effective over merely efficient.

What do I need to do/bring/wear for the event?

Nothing to do before. Come ready to learn with a growth mindset. No need to bring anything. Dress smart business casual. I’ll provide everything you need from pens & paper to tea/coffee and morning tea.

How much is it to attend and can I bring a friend?

This is a FREE event (valued at $297 pp). You can bring a BUSINESS friend. Ideally you’re in business and registered for GST; startups welcome if you’re thinking business, not hobby. If more than one is coming, please register separately so we have names, contacts, and dietary needs.

What business requirements do I need to attend?

No strict requirements, but being registered for GST helps. Startups are welcome if it’s a full-time business. We’ve had multi-outlet, multi-million revenue businesses attend. Most important: a positive, growth mindset, ready to learn.

Are you going to sell to me?

Yes, but not hard sell. This free event gives you real value first. You’ll walk away with value whether or not you do more with me. If you want next steps, I’ll share an affordable option—no pressure. I’ll even say “don’t buy” if it’s not right for you.

How many people will be in the workshop?

Between 4 to 12 people. We keep it small so you can ask questions and get tailored help. Efficient is 100 people; effective is 12. I want you to walk away with value.

I have dietary requirements?

No problem. When you register, add dietary needs (be specific—e.g., vegetarian but no eggs; milk preferences). If I’m unsure, I’ll email to confirm so we have what you need.

Where will I park?

After you register I’ll email directions. Best parking is Athol Paterson Car Park (between Lawson St and Suter St behind the Southport Library) — $8.70 full day ($3/hour). G-Link stop is close; we’re across the road on the back side of Australia Fair.
